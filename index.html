ABOUT SOUNDS LIKE NOISE

Custom music that belongs to you

Sounds Like Noise is a music and sound design production house lead by Arron Croft. We offer services such as original compositions being written for weddings, awards evenings, adverts, short films, video games, apps, and also foley and sound design for films, games, documentaries and animations.

Arron Croft


Welcome to Sounds Like Noise. Here, we know that you want audio that matches the professionalism of your own work; whether its music for adverts, music for short films or music and sound effects for video games - you deserve to be given music that has been created with the same passion and drive as the rest of your project. I've been a musician for over ten years, and has been composing for projects for the last six. During my time attending the University of Hull for both my Undergraduate degree (1st class Hons) and Masters degree, I've as become fully versed in ProTools and the operation of SSL equipped studios, as well as having studied film and game composition in both my degrees. I have worked on countless projects, the most recent of which has resulted in my name being in the Hull Maritime Museum as the Mixing Engineer and Music Editor for a piece composed by George Marshall, as part of Hull's City of Culture 2017 status.

As well as recording and playing live instruments, I'm also a skilled user of samples and different computer programmes, essential for syncing up music for video or creating interactive music for video games. A full list of these are as follows:
